# Sample Storyboard: The Future of AI in Drug Discovery
## 3-slide overview for a biotech investor audience | 15 minutes

---

## SLIDE 1: The Opportunity
**Title:** "AI Is Rewriting the Drug Discovery Playbook"

### Key Content
- Traditional drug discovery: 10-15 years, $2.6B per approved drug
- AI-driven discovery: compressing timelines by 30-50% in early stages
- 2024: 100+ AI-discovered molecules in clinical trials globally
- Key players: Recursion, Insilico Medicine, Isomorphic Labs, Relay Therapeutics

### Graphic Concept
- Bar chart comparing traditional vs. AI-assisted timelines at each stage
- Stat boxes: $2.6B (traditional cost), 100+ (AI molecules in clinic), 30-50% (time savings)

### Messaging
> "AI won't replace scientists. It will make the ones who use it dramatically more productive."

---

## SLIDE 2: Where the Value Is
**Title:** "Follow the Data"

### Key Content
- Target identification: AlphaFold solved protein structure. What's next?
- Molecule design: generative chemistry producing novel scaffolds in hours, not months
- Clinical trial optimization: AI matching patients, predicting endpoints, adaptive designs
- Real-world evidence: mining EHR data for post-market insights

### Graphic Concept
- Pie chart of AI drug discovery investment by stage
- 2x2 matrix: Maturity (early/mature) vs. Impact (incremental/transformative)

### Messaging
> "The biggest value isn't in replacing chemists -- it's in finding the targets nobody else sees."

---

## SLIDE 3: The Risks
**Title:** "What Could Go Wrong"

### Key Content
- Data quality: models are only as good as the training data
- Reproducibility: AI predictions still fail in wet lab validation ~70% of the time
- Regulatory uncertainty: FDA has no framework for AI-discovered drugs specifically
- Hype cycle: many "AI-first" biotechs have yet to deliver a Phase 3 success

### Messaging
> "Be skeptical of any company that says AI will 10x their pipeline. Be excited about companies that use AI to ask better questions."
